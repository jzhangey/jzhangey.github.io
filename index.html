General Information

I'm currently a PhD Student at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology advised by Prof. Tong Zhang. I'm now working on machine learning theory.

Before joining HKUST, I received my MPhil degree from University of Sydney in 2019 and Bachelor's Degree from USTC in 2017. During the MPhil study, I was advised by Prof. Dacheng Tao on machine learning. I also worked as an intern of Prof. Zhaoran Wang's Group at the IEMS, the Northwester University before.
